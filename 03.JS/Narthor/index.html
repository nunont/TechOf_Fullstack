Checklist app

Keep track of your daily tasks!

How does it work?

  • Enter your task on the Tasks Input square
  • Press "Add Task" to add it on a List below
  • You can check your tasks as Completed by clicking on them
  • If you wish to delete a task from your List, press the "X" button at the end of it
  • You can also store your list with a Custom Name! After adding all of your tasks, enter a name on the List Name Input square and press the "Save List" button to keep it!
